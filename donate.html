THE INTEGRATED-APPROACH

The Centre delivers into four major programmes which are defined by their intended outcomes; agriculture, education, environment and health. Each programme addresses major issues in their respective sector affecting populations across the world. All thematic areas match their focus on technology and innovation with a parallel commitment to making a difference in communities by employing diverse combination of technology, partnerships, training and communications to encourage adoption of new technologies and improve communities.
