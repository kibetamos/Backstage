Tree Planting Programme

Conserve the Environemnt by planting trees

The Centre is on an a mission to plant 8 million trees by 2030, engaging community participation as one of the key strategies to promote sustainable forests and converse the environment.

Among the Projects we have include

    Fruit Tree Planting in schools
    Bio-gas project
    Bivalves and Seaweeds aquaculture
    Decentralised Waste Water Plant in Tackling Water Scarcity and Ameliorate Agricultural
    Bamboo Plantations to Rehabilitate Athi-River and Turkwel River from Degradation
